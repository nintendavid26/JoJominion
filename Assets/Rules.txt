JoJoMinion:
Rules:
Setup: 10 card piles, 5 2-4 cost, 3 5-7 cost, 2 8-10 cost.
	   Each player starts with 100 HP, A 10 card deck (7 coins, 3 punches)
	   
Gameplay:
	Each turn a player may play any action cards, and buy 1 card.
	Cards that deal damage causes the opponent to lose HP.
	If the other person's HP reaches 0 you win.
